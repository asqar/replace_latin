Qazaqstan, tolyq atawy Qazaqstan Respwblikasy — Shyghys Ewropa men Ortalyq Azijada ornalasqan memleket. Batysynda Edilding toemengi aghysynan, shyghysynda Altaj tawlaryna dejin 3000 km-ge, soltuestiktegi Batys Sibir zhazyghynan, ongtuestiktegi Qyzylqum shoeli men Tjan‘-Shan‘ taw zhuejesine 1 600 km-ge sozylyp zhatyr. Qazaqstan Kaspij tengizi arqyly Aezirbajzhan, Iran elderine, Edil oezeni zhaene Edil-Don kanaly arqyly Azov zhaene Qara tengizderge shygha alady. Muhitqa tikelej shygha almajtyn memleketterding ishinde Qazaqstan - eng uelkeni.

Qazaqstan bes memleketpen shekaralas, sonyng ishinde aelemdegi qurlyqtaghy eng uzaq shekara soltuestiginde zhəne batysynda Resejmen — 6 467 km qurajdy. Ongtuestiginde — Tuerikmenstan — 380 km, Oezbekstan — 2 300 km zhaene Qyrghyzstan — 980 km, al shyghysynda — Qytajmen — 1 460 km shektesedi. Zhalpy qurlyq shekarasynyng uzyndyghy — 13392,6 km. Batysta Kaspij tengizimen, ongtuestik batysta Aral tengizimen shajylady.

2017 zhyldyng 1 qangtargha eldegi turghyndar sany - 17 926 500 adam bul aelem bojynsha 64-oryn. Zher koelemi zhaghynan aelem elderining ishinde 9-oryn alady (2 724 900 km²).

Elding astanasy - Astana qalasy. Memlekettik tili - qazaq tili. Resmi tili - orys tili.

Qazaqstannyng ulttyq quramy alwan tuerli. Halyqtyng uelken boeligin qazaqtar qurajdy, pajyzdyq uelesi - 66,01%, orystar - 21,05%, oezbekter - 3,07%, wkraindar - 1,70%, ujghyrlar - 1,44%, tatarlar - 1,17%, basqa halyqtar - 5,56%. Halyqtyng 70 pajyzdan astamyn musylmandar qurajdy, pravoslavty hristiandar - 26%, qalghany basqa din oekilderi.

Ekonomikalyq koersetkishteri bojynsha damwshy ekonomika retinde qarastyrylady. Elding zhalpy ishki oenimi ZhIOe (nominal) = $225.619 mlrd (2014). Ekonomikanyng negizgi baghyty otyn-energetika salasyndaghy shikizat oendirw, awyl sharwashylyghy (eginshilik). Elding negizgi valjutasy - tengge.

1991 zhyldyng 16 zheltoqsan kueni KSRO-nyng ydyrawyna bajlanysty oez egemendigin zharijalady zhaene halyqaralyq qawymdastyq tarapynan taewelsiz memleket retinde mojyndaldy. 1992 zhyldyng 2 nawryzynan bastap BUU-nyng tolyqqandy mueshesi. Sonymen qatar Ewropadaghy qawipsizdik zhaene yntymaqtastyq ujymyna, Uzhymdyq qawipsizdik kelisimi ujymyna, Shanhaj Yntymaqtastyq Ujymyna zhaene Ewrazijalyq Ekonomikalyq Qawymdastyghy sijaqty basqa da birneshe halyqaralyq ujymdardyng quramyna kiredi.
